LeveLog Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

LeveLog ("we", "our", "the app") is a construction compliance and daily logging tool for NYC DOB-regulated jobsites. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, company, role, and password (stored hashed).
  • Jobsite data: daily logs, OSHA logbook entries, DOB permit details, subcontractor orientation records, worker check-ins, and signatures you enter.
  • Photos: images you capture or attach to reports via your device camera or photo library.
  • Location: approximate or precise location, only when you explicitly geotag a report or check-in.
  • NFC tag IDs: identifiers from physical check-in tags you scan.
  • Device info: app version, operating system version, and crash diagnostics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality (logs, reports, check-ins, compliance tracking).
  • To synchronize your data across devices you own.
  • To generate compliance reports (OSHA, DOB) that you choose to create.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.

3. Permissions

  • Camera: to capture photos for daily logs and incident reports.
  • Photo library / storage: to attach existing images to reports.
  • Location: to geotag reports and worker check-ins (only when you trigger the action).
  • NFC: to read worker check-in tags.
  • Internet / network state: to sync data to your company account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers. Data is accessible to:

  • Users within your company account (according to role-based permissions you configure).
  • Our hosting and infrastructure providers (e.g., Render, MongoDB Atlas, Dropbox) solely to operate the service.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us (see Contact below). Construction compliance records required by law (e.g., OSHA 300 logs) may be retained in read-only form for the legally mandated period.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed. Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access is controlled by authenticated sessions and role-based permissions.

7. Children's Privacy

LeveLog is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, email: privacy@levelog.com
